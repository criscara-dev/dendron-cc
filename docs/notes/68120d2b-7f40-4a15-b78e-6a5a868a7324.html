Reference

What is Isometric Stretching? Isometric Stretching: Tense strongly for a few seconds, relax, stretch within th first second of relaxation, tense again. Keep this cycle of tension and relaxation until you cannot stretch any more without pain. Hold the last tension to strengthen your muscles at their greatest stretch.
