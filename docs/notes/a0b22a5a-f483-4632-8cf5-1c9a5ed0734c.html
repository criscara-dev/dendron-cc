Time blocking

Productive people get more done, achieve better results, and earn far more in their hours than the rest. They do so because they devote maximum time to being productive on their top priority, their ONE Thing. ==They time block their ONE Thing and then protect their time blocks with a vengeance.==

    flowchart LR
    in[time block your One thing] --> out[Protect your time block]

Wrong below

If disproportionate results come from one activity, then you must give that one activity disproportionate time

Correct below

  1. Block time as early in your day as you possibly can.
  2. Time block your time off.
  3. Time block your ONE Thing.
  4. Time block your planning time.
  5. Don't block more than 4 hours a day.

Don't work on time, like it's 5 PM. Let's go home! Work based on event: "My work is done. Let's go home"

Efficiency is doing things right. ==Effectiveness== is doing the right thing.
